Food Storage Tips

Refrigerator Organization

Store ready-to-eat foods on top shelves, raw ingredients on bottom shelves to prevent cross-contamination.

Freezing Guidelines

Label containers with contents and date. Most frozen food maintains quality for 3-6 months.

Fruit & Vegetable Care

Store most fruits and vegetables in high humidity drawers. Keep bananas, apples, and tomatoes separate.

Pantry Management

Use the FIFO method (First In, First Out) to rotate products and utilize older items first.
