Food Donation Guidelines

What to Donate

  • Fresh fruits and vegetables
  • Unopened packaged foods
  • Canned goods (not dented or damaged)
  • Freshly prepared meals
  • Baked goods (within 2 days of baking)

What Not to Donate

  • Food past its expiration date
  • Partially consumed items
  • Food with signs of spoilage
  • Home-canned goods
  • Food stored at unsafe temperatures
